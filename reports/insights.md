# Financial Market Pricing Analysis — Business Insights

## Executive Summary
This project reviewed synthetic financial market pricing data to identify pricing exceptions, large price movements, missing vendor prices, and possible stale prices. The analysis supports the type of pricing review used in financial operations, investment operations, and FinTech environments.

## Key Findings
- Most pricing records were successfully reviewed without exceptions.
- A smaller group of records required review due to large market movements, missing vendor prices, or possible stale prices.
- Vendor-level review helped identify which pricing sources had higher exception counts.
- Asset class analysis showed that some instruments had more pricing movement than others.
- Large daily price movements should be reviewed to confirm whether they are valid market changes or possible pricing errors.

## Business Recommendations
- Review all records marked as “Needs Review” before final reporting.
- Monitor vendors with higher exception rates.
- Investigate missing prices quickly to reduce valuation and reporting risk.
- Use exception dashboards to help operations teams prioritize daily review work.
- Keep historical exception tracking to identify repeated vendor or security-level issues.
